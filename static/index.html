Cast media protocol compliancy tests

A test suite to ensure that your receiver app is compliant with Googles Cast Media protocol

Instructions

Does your receiver app require custom authentication?

Please follow the below procedure to test Cast Media protocol compliancy

  1. Launch your sender app on Android or iOS
  2. Connect to your Chromecast
  3. Play a piece of media
  4. Pause the media
  5. Enter your AppID below and click "Start" to begin testing

Please follow the below procedure to test Cast Media protocol compliancy

  1. Enter the URL of your publicly accessible media
  2. Select the meta content type of your media
  3. Enter your AppID, then click "Start" to begin testing
